The Rainmaker in Insurance Marketing

More than twenty-five years ago, Larry and Jeff Neilson founded Neilson Marketing Services to provide the insurance industry with specialized marketing services that spoke to its needs and the specific space in which each stakeholder focused. From the beginning, we defined how insurance telemarketing would succeed and set the gold standard in this arena. We capitalized on what data could bring to insurance marketing to help agencies reach new business consumers, and assist MGAs, wholesalers and insurers to expand their distribution system. And we were among the first to leverage the Internet for online marketing for agencies, MGAs, wholesalers, program administrators, and insurance vendors.

Today, Neilson Marketing continues to disrupt the way things are done, delivering the latest in high-performance, result-oriented insurance marketing solutions to the industry we exclusively serve.
